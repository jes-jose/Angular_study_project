Tropical Lagoon

Cusine:Hawaian
Serving:1
Mode Of Cooking:Easy

Preparation time:

5 minutes

Cooking time:

0 minutes

Cooking Instructions

  1. Fill a hurricane glass or tall glass with ice
  2. Add Malibu Rum and Blue Curacao to glass
